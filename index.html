How do we structure the day?

1. Training
We teach all the necessary things to jump from the plane without any kind of problem.

2. Get Ready
You are already prepared, you just need the suir and a parachute. All sizes available.

3. Fly
You are ready, ant the plane is waiting for us in the hangar. Let's fly!

4. Jump!
You have done the most complicated. Just one step left... jump!
